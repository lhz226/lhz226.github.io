About HDMCT

Providing comprehensive automation solutions since 2005

Your Partner in Industrial Automation

Dongguan Hangda Industrial Measurement and Control Technology (HDMCT) is a company specializing in production informatization, production automation and material automation. We provide fully automated, highly production-informatized automation solutions based on automation monitoring systems, pneumatic conveying systems and weighing batching systems, combined with production information systems.

Our service scope includes project design, equipment production, on-site installation, debugging and training. We look forward to providing you with customized material automation solutions.

With nearly two decades of experience, HDMCT has become a trusted partner for numerous enterprises in chemical, building materials, food processing, pharmaceutical and other manufacturing sectors.

Hopper Weighing System

Advanced gravimetric measurement technology for bulk materials with industry-leading ±0.05% accuracy.

  • Stainless steel construction for harsh environments
  • Multi-stage filtration for dust control
  • Real-time data logging and analytics
  • Custom capacities from 5kg to 50 tons

Vacuum Conveying & Batching System

Fully automated material handling with precision batch control and contamination-free transfer.

  • Gentle material handling preserves product integrity
  • Integrated CIP (Clean-in-Place) functionality
  • Energy-efficient vortex vacuum technology
  • Throughput up to 15 tons/hour

Manual Batching System

Flexible, cost-effective solution for small batch production with precision control.

  • Ergonomic operator interface
  • Recipe management for up to 500 formulations
  • Optional barcode/RFID verification
  • Modular design for easy expansion

Liquid Dosing System

Precision volumetric or gravimetric dosing for liquids with viscosity up to 50,000 cP.

  • ±0.1% dosing accuracy
  • Hygienic design with FDA-compliant materials
  • Self-cleaning nozzle technology
  • Flow rates from 1ml/min to 500l/min

Powder Dosing System

Advanced loss-in-weight technology for precise powder and granule dispensing.

  • Anti-bridging and anti-caking technology
  • Micro-dosing capability down to 0.1g
  • Pneumatic or screw feeder options
  • ATEX certification for explosive environments

Multi-Component Dosing

Synchronized dosing of up to 12 components with perfect ratio control.

  • Dynamic ratio adjustment during operation
  • Automatic viscosity compensation
  • Integrated static mixer options
  • Real-time component consumption tracking
